# Week 2: Data Visualization

Week 2 saw us focus on **Tableau** and **Power BI** as a way of visualising data and being able to share it publicly.

---

### Day 1
On Day 1 we focused on **Tableau**, learning about how it is a significantly stronger visualisation tool than Excel. 

* **Task:** Performed our first basic data analysis.
* **Outcome:** Created column charts and map visuals.

### Day 2 
Day 2 saw us focus on two large data sets: **Spotify** and **Global Health**. 

* **Analysis:** Practised creating visuals and analysis such as $R$ scores. 
* **Insights:** Explored why relationships (like BMI and Life Expectancy) differed across separate continents. 
* **Reflection:** As someone who previously used Excel, the ability to easily create visuals and control the data shown was very promising.

### Day 3
Day 3 saw us learn about **Power BI**. While similar to Tableau, I quickly realised just how powerful a tool it is. 

* **Data Cleaning:** Learnt how to clean messy data to ensure usability. 
* **Features:** Focused on creating graphs, adding hierarchies, and using pivot tables to control data selection and visuals in one place.

### Day 4
Day 4 was the most important as we learnt how to create **online dashboards**. In the modern age, where teams work across different systems, this is vital for ensuring good teamwork.
